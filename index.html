How to Make Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta; any kind
  • 1 cup sliced bell pepper
  • 1 cup sliced Zucchini
  • 1 cup sliced tomatoes
  • 1/4 sliced banana peppers
  • 1/4 olives
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese or any kind
  • 1/3 fresh basil

Homemade Dressing

  • 1/3 red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions

  • 1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook until tender, 6 to 10 minutes (check the package for recommended cook time). Drain and rinse well.
  • 2.While the pasta cooks, in the bottom of a large bowl, make the dressing. Whisk the red wine vinegar, salt, pepper, oregano, pepperoncini juice (if using), and the olive oil until blended. Add the drained and rinsed pasta to the dressing and mix well.
  • 3.Stir in the bell pepper, zucchini, tomatoes, scallions, pepperoncini (if using), olives, parmesan, mozzarella, and the herbs (if using). Taste for seasoning and adjust with salt and pepper as needed. Serve or for the best results.
